3. Synthesis of the Practicum Engagement

During my internship at the Biñan Government as an IT intern, my group and I were assigned to update and test the existing web portal created by previous interns from Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna (MMCL). This task allowed me to enhance my skills in PHP and learn to work with the MVC (Model-View-Controller) framework, which was new to me. We also used various online resources to troubleshoot issues and improve the portal’s performance, which made me realize how valuable the internet is as a learning and development tool. One of the biggest challenges I faced was managing my time effectively, as I was also working on our capstone project alongside my practicum. This experience taught me how to balance multiple responsibilities, meet deadlines, and stay productive despite the pressure. Overall, the practicum helped me grow not just technically, but also professionally. It gave me insight into how IT solutions are applied in real-world government settings and prepared me for future roles in the industry.
